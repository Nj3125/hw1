The Beauty of New England

When thinking about the regions of the US, one may think at first
about the of the roar of New York City, the towering Rocky mountains
that comprise the spine of continental North America, or the scale of the
adequately named Grand Canyon. However, many people do not know of the many
wonders found in New England. This website contains a couple of photos I
have taken while on vacation, all in the areas comprising or surrounding
New England.
